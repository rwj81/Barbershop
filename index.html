Specialties

Haircuts and styles: men's tapering, layering, fades, shaves, high & tights, clean-ups, outlines, and hot lather neck shaves. Beard trims, facial shaves. Children welcome too.

History

Established in 1951. Still operating in the same location in the heart of Saint Matthews. Current proprietor Doris Held has owned for 21 years.
