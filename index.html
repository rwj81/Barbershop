Specialties

Haircuts and styles: men's tapering, layering, fades, shaves, high & tights, clean-ups, outlines, and hot later neck shaves. Beard trims, face shaves, facial massages. Children and women welcome.

History

Established in 1951. Still operating in the same location in the heart of Saint Matthews. Current proprietor Doris Held has owned for 21 years.
